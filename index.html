Usage Policy
Before we begin.
Oracle is a tool for ethical decision-making. Read and agree to proceed.
✕
No harmful intent. Do not ask how to harm, threaten, or hurt any person or group.
e.g. "How do I hurt someone" → Blocked immediately
✕
No illegal activities. Do not ask for advice on robbery, theft, fraud, hacking, or any criminal act.
e.g. "Should I rob a bank or hack a system" → Blocked immediately
✕
No weapons or violence. Do not request guidance on acquiring, building, or using weapons to cause harm.
e.g. "Should I buy an illegal weapon" → Blocked immediately
✕
No manipulation or coercion. Do not use Oracle to plan psychological manipulation, blackmail, or coercion of others.
e.g. "How do I manipulate my friend into doing X" → Blocked immediately
✕
No self-harm or dangerous behavior. Do not ask for advice that could lead to personal harm or endangerment of yourself or others.
e.g. "Should I take this dangerous drug" → Blocked immediately
✓
Real decisions only — all topics welcome. Career, business, finance, relationships, education, health, technology — if it's a real decision with real options, Oracle can help.
e.g. "Should I quit my job, start a business, or go back to school?" → Perfect use case
Violations are detected automatically and will block your request. Repeated violations may terminate your session. Oracle is powered by AI — always apply your own judgment to critical decisions.
